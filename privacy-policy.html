Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our augmented reality (AR) game, which is intended for children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with applicable laws including COPPA and Google Play Families Policy.

2. Permissions We Use

Camera

The app uses your device’s camera to enable augmented reality gameplay. We do not record, store, or transmit camera images or video. Camera access is used only in real time.

Location

The app uses location data to provide location-based gameplay features. Location is used only while the app is in use and is not stored or shared. We do not track users over time.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads.

For child users:

  • Only non-personalized ads are shown
  • No behavioral tracking is used
  • Child-directed treatment is enabled

AdMob may collect limited information such as device type and non-personal identifiers to provide contextual ads.

Learn more:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Collection

We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, photos, or stored location data. Only limited non-personal data may be used for app functionality and advertising.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • Authorities if required by law

6. Parental Controls

Parents or guardians can:

  • Disable camera or location permissions via device settings
  • Control ad preferences through device settings

If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will remove it.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method is completely secure.
